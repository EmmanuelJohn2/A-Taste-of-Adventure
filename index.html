Hello and Welcome to A Taste of Adventure!

A Taste of Adventure is a blogsite that showcases varying delicacies that originate from the first few regions in the Philippines are known for as well as the recipes and ingredients needed to make them. Our goal in making this website is to catalogue some of the most unique dishes of a given region in hopes of allowing more people to be made aware of their unique and wonderful cuisine.
