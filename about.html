Purpose

The Philippines has a rich history filled with encounters from various races from all over the world. While that does contribute to the diversity in the types of food available to the Filipino masses, we also feel that the large amounts of influence from international cuisines have made our native dishes, born from the meticulous culinary techniques and experiences unique only to the people of that region and time frame, feel more like an after thought instead of what we consider as one of the things we proudly associate with our nation’s culture.

As such, we’ve sought to create a space where Filipinos can post and share the dishes their descendants grew up making, ensuring that their dishes, and by extension the culture of their people, will be not lost to time and instead reach people of all walks of life, for generations to come.
